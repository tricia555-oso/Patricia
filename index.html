Academic Focus

Why do I study International Business?

In a world that is more connected than ever, understanding how business operates across borders is not just an academic pursuit — it is a superpower. International Business gives me the frameworks to think globally while acting locally, to understand trade, commerce, culture, and strategy in ways that are directly applicable to building ventures right here in Sierra Leone and beyond. As an entrepreneur, every lesson learned in the classroom becomes a tool I deploy in the real world. That alignment between study and practice is what makes this field deeply personal to me.

Vision

Career Goals

My primary ambition is to build businesses that create meaningful employment and economic opportunity in Sierra Leone and across West Africa. I aspire to grow as a business strategist and coach — empowering young entrepreneurs, especially women, to launch and scale ventures with confidence. Through my ongoing work at Innovation SL and my studies in international business, I am laying the groundwork for a career that sits at the intersection of enterprise, mentorship, and social impact. I believe that entrepreneurship is one of the most powerful forces for transformation, and I intend to dedicate my career to proving exactly that.
